19th Baja SAE Brasil Championship

As a hookie competitor, i've worked with some manufacturing and maintenance function delivered from the team. it was such a landmark for my growth as a learner, when I was having my first contact with the industry and the maker movement. My contact with the competitions, which have the purpose of simulate the engineering market, designing and manufacturing a prototype for a business environment, has made me a bigger professional, once I could be in touch with team work. From technical design to manufacturing, solving problems and learning by practice, often under pressure. Como competidor novato, cumpri as funções de fabricação e manutenção exigidas pela equipe. Foi um grande marco no meu aprendizado, quando começou meu contato com a indústria e com o mundo maker. O contato com as competições Baja SAE, que vizam simular o funcionamento de uma pequena empresa projetista e fabricante de um protótipo para apresentação no mercado, me tornou um profissional mais completo, uma vez que pude experimentar o trabalho em equipe em todas as suas formas.
